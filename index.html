Portfolio

PhotoSpotter

PhotoSpotter is an interactive Web App that gives users the power to recreate their favorite pictures by giving them the location, time and camera information of the image, as well as local weather. The user can input the subject they would like to photograph and instantly be transported to a gallery of their specified interest. With one click of an image, the user will immediately receive all the necessary information to create a similar photo.

Lender-Be

A "social lending" site. Users can list any items they have that they are willing to lend. Friends, family, and neighbors can connect and can search to see if someone has something they need, from a book to a power tool. People can request to borrow and, if accepted, then there is a record of who has the item, a field where the borrower puts how long they need it, another where the lender can put when they need it back, and the app can send reminders of the "due date."
